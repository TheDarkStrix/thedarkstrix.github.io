    5 Reasons To Use A Website Builder From WestHost

    Kelly Kirkham| @kk |May 23, 2023 4:56 PM

    Welcome to Website Builder!

    WestHost clients have the option to easily drag-and-drop their way to website success with our easy-to-use Website Builder. Simply choose your Website Builder package and follow the directions to checkout or, for existing WestHost clients, add Website Builder to your CHI account.

    Why hire a team of developers when you can easily save time and money by building a professional, optimized website yourself?

    If you’re still not sure that Website Builder is for you, see our top five reasons for building your own website or online shop with WestHost below:

    #1. Automatic Website Responsiveness

    Your website will automatically look great on any sized screen. Website Builder is optimized so that you don’t have to struggle with stressful coding or programming. Instead, you simply drag and drop your website elements into place, press ‘Publish’, and your website is streamlined and efficient no matter what device is used to view. Not only will your website visitors be pleased, but your website will also get a boost in search engine results since Google and Bing reward mobile-optimized websites.

    #2. Free Templates

    Website Builder offers 165 hand-designed templates to choose from completely free of charge. Simply choose the template that catches your eye and start building your new website. Ecommerce packages include a hassle-free shopping cart, complete with PCI DSS compliance to keep your shoppers safe. Instantly boost brand reputation with additional security features to let customers know that you value their data security.

    #3. Free Web Address

    Every Website Builder package from WestHost includes a free web address or domain name. Domain names are as unique as your website, meaning that no other website can use your web address. Help your website traffic easily find you through a domain name that matches your brand. Find the perfect domain name for your website with our domain search tool.

    #4. Fully Customizable

    Don’t limit your creativity with restrictive website tools. Website Builder from WestHost allows you to customize your website until it meets your expectations in every way. Link to social media accounts to help customers share your amazing products and services. Add a blog and other web pages to share industry knowledge with the world. Need to make website changes on the go? No problem! You can customize your website from your smartphone anywhere you have an internet connection.

    #5. Budget-Friendly

    Website Builder packages from WestHost begin at less than $5 per month. For just a few pennies a day, you can have a fully-functional, fully-customizable website to help reach an entirely new audience. Save thousands of dollars by investing an hour of your time to build your own website. Your experience will be completely stress-free, and if you have any questions along the way, our expert support team is available 24/7 to answer any questions you may have.
